-- The ones digit of a sequence of multiplications is the product of the ones digits of all the numbers in the multiplication.
-- So we need to calculate $(1 \cdot 3 \cdot 5 \cdot 7 \cdot 9 \cdot 11 \cdot 13) \bmod 10$.
calc
  1 * 3 * 5 * 7 * 9 * 11 * 13 % 10
    = (1 * 3 * 5 * 7 * 9 * 11 * 13) % 10 := by rfl
  _ = 15015 % 10 := by norm_num
  _ = 5 := by norm_num
