\[ \frac{3}{2} \div 3 = \frac{3}{2} \times \frac{1}{3} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2} \]
Thus, the equation becomes:
\[ \frac{1}{2} = \frac{x}{10} \]
Next, solve for \( x \) by cross-multiplying:
\[ 10 \times \frac{1}{2} = x \]
